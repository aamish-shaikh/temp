ABOUT US

Riyadiya Group Marketing Management EST specializes in propelling your brand and its distinctive image to the forefront of targeted markets in the Middle East, North Africa, and Asia through proactive engagement with retailers and consumers. Our global teams, strategically positioned around the world, possess the expertise to seamlessly unite brands, retailers, and consumers.


Founded in 2003, our company's headquarters is centrally located in Dubai. As a privately owned entity, RIYADIYA GROUP extends its reach with branches across the MENA and Asian regions, boasting a dedicated workforce of over 300 employees, all directly affiliated with our organization. Additionally, we maintain state-of-the-art warehousing facilities in both the UAE and KSA.


Riyadiya Group's operational footprint spans a multitude of countries, including the United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Jordan, Lebanon, Morocco, Tunisia, Algeria, Egypt, Pakistan, and Malaysia. We are committed to delivering innovative marketing solutions and fostering impactful connections across diverse markets.
